NEO's

NEO's (Near Earth Objects) are asteriods and comets that have been directed towards Earth by our neighboring planet's gravitational force. NEO's do not enter the Earth's atmoshpere, however, if one were too, it would become a Metorite.

Metorites

Metorites are asteriods or comets that have entered the Earth's atmosphere. The majority of Metorites burn up when entering the atmoshpere but a few manage to make it to the Earth's surface. Most Metorite are considered Micrometeorites and tend to be dust particles or very small in size.
